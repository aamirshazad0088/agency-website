Who We Are

We Build AI Software That Does Meaningful Work

Zorex is an AI software agency. We design, build, and integrate custom AI systems — agentic applications, LLM-powered tools, and intelligent integrations — for growing businesses that want to acquire more clients and move fast without buying generic software.

Our Mission

We work with companies ranging from 10-person teams to 500-person organizations across a range of industries. Everything we build is scoped to your specific context, your data, and your operational reality to ensure you get a measurable return on investment through increased acquisition and reduced operational drag.

How We Staff Your Project

Every project is led by an experienced AI systems architect and supported by specialists matched to your technical requirements. We don't outsource or offshore your core build. You work with the people who designed the system.
