Who We Are

We Build AI Systems That Run the Work You Shouldn't Be Doing.

Zorex is an AI software agency. We design, build, and integrate custom AI agentic applications, LLM-powered tools, and intelligent automations for growing businesses that want to scale without scaling their headcount.

Why We Exist

At some point, every growing business hits the same wall.

The work that got you here the manual processes, the spreadsheets, the "someone will handle that" starts costing more than it's worth. Your best people are doing work a system should be doing. Your margins are funding inefficiency you can't see but definitely feel.

We built Zorex for that moment.

Not to sell you software. Not to hand you a tool and wish you luck. But to sit down with how your business actually runs and build something that fixes the exact thing that's slowing it down.

How We Staff Your Project

Every project is led by an experienced AI systems architect and supported by specialists matched to your technical requirements. We don't outsource or offshore your core build. You work with the people who designed the system.
